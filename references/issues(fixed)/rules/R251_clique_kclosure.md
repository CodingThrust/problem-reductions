## Reduction Algorithm

> INSTANCE: Directed graph G = (V,A), positive integer K ≤ |V|.
> QUESTION: Is there a subset V' ⊆ V with |V'| ≤ K such that for all (u,v) ∈ A either u ∈ V' or v ∉ V'?
