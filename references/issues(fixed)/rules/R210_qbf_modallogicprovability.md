## GJ Source Entry

> [LO14] MODAL LOGIC PROVABILITY (*)
> INSTANCE: Well-formed modal formula A, modal system S E {K,T,S4} (see reference or [Hughes and Cresswell, 1968] for details of K, T, and S4).
> QUESTION: Is A provable in system S?
> Reference: [Ladner, 1977]. Transformation from QBF.
> Comment: PSPACE-complete for fixed S E {K,T,S4} or for any fixed modal system S in which everything provable in K, but nothing not provable in S4, can be proved.
